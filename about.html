About Us

Welcome to House of Brands, a global leader in the watch and accessories industry, with over 23 years of expertise in design, manufacturing, and sourcing. With a strong presence across India, the UK, and the UAE, we specialize in curating premium timepieces, fashion accessories, eyewear, and lifestyle products that cater to evolving market trends and consumer preferences.

At House of Brands, we pride ourselves on being a trusted OEM partner for leading global brands and top e-commerce platforms, including Myntra, Flipkart, Noon, Reliance, FirstCry, and many more across India, KSA, and the UAE. Our deep-rooted understanding of product development, design innovation, and supply chain management allows us to deliver high-quality products at competitive prices, meeting the demands of both emerging and established brands.

With state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, a dedicated team of designers and engineers, and a robust sourcing network across Asia and Europe, we ensure precision, quality control, and timely delivery at every stage of production. Our expertise lies in watches, bracelets, sunglasses, leather accessories, and gifting solutions, making us a one-stop destination for brands seeking customized and innovative product lines.

Our Strengths:

  • 23 years of industry experience
  • Global offices in India, UK & UAE
  • Strong partnerships with top e-commerce platforms in India, KSA & UAE
  • Expertise in product development, manufacturing, and private labeling
  • Innovative design team and advanced production facilities
  • High-quality sourcing network across Asia and Europe

At House of Brands, we are not just manufacturers; we are brand builders and market leaders, helping brands scale and thrive in competitive markets. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has allowed us to build long-lasting relationships with some of the biggest names in the industry.

Whether you're looking for custom-designed watches, stylish accessories, or premium gifting solutions, House of Brands is your trusted partner for end-to-end sourcing and manufacturing solutions.

We aim to redefine style, craftsmanship, and innovation as we continue to expand our reach and establish House of Brands as a global powerhouse in the fashion and lifestyle industry.
